How to buy

Download & setup MetaMask or TrustWallet

Download MetaMask (a crypto wallet in form of a browser extension) or TrustWallet (an app for your phone). After that you will have to add the Binance Smart Chain to your network-list.(Click here for a step-by-step tutorial)

Buy and send BNB to MetaMask

Buy BNB on an exchange (i.e. Binance, Kraken, Coinbase etc.). Transfer the tokens to your MetaMask wallet address. BEP-20 addresses start with a "0x"

Head on to PancakeSwap and swap for $BWT

head to PancakeSwap, Set the slippage tolerance to 15% (sometimes it may be a bit more, depending on how much demand there is)

View $BWT and HODL

Swap BNB for $BWT. Now you need to add $BWT to your MetaMask or Trust Wallet to view your $BWT. Lastly HODL and enjoy the BNBs!

2 Will my rewards always be paid hourly?

Our unique reward system is triggered by volume (buy + sell activity to cover gas fees). Payouts to holders may not occur every hour if trading volume is low. When this is the case, the amount of rewarded BNB each holder will receive does not decrease. However, will accrue over a longer period of time until it is suitable for a payout to trigger (usually a few hours).
